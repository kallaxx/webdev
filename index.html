What is MBTI?

MBTI stands for Myers Briggs Type Indicator. It is based on the study on cognitive functions by Carl Jung. With the knowledge from this paper, Katherine Cook Briggs and Isabel Briggs Myers had developed this test, as a simplified version of the original study. The aim of this is to help people to understand themselves and others with more detail. The results can help you improve your everyday life, ranging from studying, working, to socializing. It is also a great way to practice some self-reflection and might also help you accept yourself and overcome your less desirable traits, by giving them meaning.
